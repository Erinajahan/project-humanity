Our Donate Plan

Our donate plan allocated in three section.
First for the food, Second for the health and medicine & third for the shelter

Food for the people

Food is the most essential requirement for sustenance of human life.Even if a human being does not have shelter over their head or clothes over their body, they would still survive if they get wholesome nutrition.So we are going to provide food and fresh water.

Medicine for the people

Now emergency Medicine is very important. Here have been thousands of recorded cases of diseases and injuries, including Acute Watery Diarrhoea.Many people have died through this situation.so we provide necessary medicines for the flood affected people.

shelter for the people

Now millions of people are homeless and without any belonging. With homes crushed, objects floating away and people missing, the country is at high alert. Even many people lost their last hope. We will provide emergency shelter for them.
